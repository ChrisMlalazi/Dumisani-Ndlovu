"...my artwork takes a critical view of social and cultural issues..."

Born in 1969 in Lupane, Zimbabwe, Dumisani NDlovu currently works from the National Gallery of Zimbabwe in Bulawayo in a printmaking studio. From 1991 to 1993 he trained in drawing and painting at the Mzilikazi Art and Craft Centre Bulawayo. Most of his work is collagraph – which is printing onto paper using a pressure press to transfer the design from a plate onto the paper, which also gives a textural relief to the design if he has used glue or objects to create the relief. .
